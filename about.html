Our Story

ToolLab is brought to you by Bramha Intelligence, a Nashik-based technology startup specializing in intelligent software solutions.

Founded by Vijay (MCA, KKWagh College), Bramha Intelligence focuses on creating practical technology that solves real-world problems. ToolLab represents our commitment to giving back to the community by offering professional-grade tools completely free of charge.

Our journey began with a simple observation: why are basic digital tools hidden behind paywalls and forced registrations? We believe everyone deserves access to quality tools without barriers.
