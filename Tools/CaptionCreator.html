Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI caption generator work?
Our AI analyzes your post description and generates creative, engaging captions tailored to your chosen platform and tone. It considers current trends and best practices for each social media platform.
Can I customize the generated captions?
Yes! You can edit the generated captions directly before copying them. You can also regenerate new options if you're not satisfied with the results.
Are the hashtags relevant to my content?
Absolutely! The AI generates hashtags based on your post description and chosen platform, ensuring they're relevant and trending for maximum reach.
Is my content stored or used for training?
No, we value your privacy. Your post descriptions and generated captions are never stored on our servers or used to train AI models.
Can I use this for business accounts?
Yes! Our caption generator works perfectly for both personal and business accounts across all social media platforms.
